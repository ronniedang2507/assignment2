Popular Restaurants in Melbourne

Below are 6 restaurants/dine in places in Melbourne that are popular. These restaurants are unique in their crusine and the way they make food.

Grill'd

Grill'd is a restaurant that serves mainly burgers. They have chips, drinks and all types of options.

Main Dishes: Burgers

Location: Melbourne Central, Level 3 La Trobe St, Melbourne VIC 3000

Mcdonalds

This is a very popular food brand chain around the world. Mcdonalds known for being a overall popular fast food chain that is always busy.

Main Dishes: Fast food, burgers, fries

Location: 275 Swanston St, Melbourne VIC 3000

Sofia

Sofia is an italian restaurant that are known for their pizza and pasta dishes. The dishes are large in portions and food is fantastic for italian crusine.

Main Dishes: Pizza and pasta

Location: 857 Burke Rd, Camberwell VIC 3124

DoDee Paidang

Dodee Paidang is a restuarant that serves amazing Thai food and Thai crusine from mouth watering papaya salads to laksa soups.

Main Dishes: Thai cruisine

Location: Basement/353 Little Collins St, Melbourne VIC 3000

Hungry Jack's

Hungry Jack's also know as burger king is a fast food restaurant that serves mainly burgers. They are mainly known for their great tasting burgers.

Main Dishes: Burgers

Location: 260 La Trobe St, Melbourne VIC 3000

KFC

KFC is another fast food chain but this one is mostly known for their fried chicken wings and drumsticks. Especially combo and boxed meals to eat with friends and family.

Main Dishes: Fried Chicken

Location: Lower Ground, Coop's Shot Tower, Swanston St, Melbourne VIC 3000
